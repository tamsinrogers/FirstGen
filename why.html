why act now?


Right now, it feels our country is going backwards. Suddenly, xenophobia and racism have stepped into the limelight. It's our duty to stand together and help one another to create a more equitable and diverse United States. We could sit here and write essays worth of points on why we should act now, but honestly all change has to start with you. Organize your friends, contribute in your community, call your representatives, start small, you never know who's life you will change.
